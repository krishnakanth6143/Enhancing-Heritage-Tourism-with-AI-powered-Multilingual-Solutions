Frequently Asked Questions

How does the translation feature work?
Our translation feature utilizes advanced Deep Translators technology to provide real-time translations of heritage information. Simply select your preferred language from the dropdown menu on any monument or site page, and the content will be instantly translated.
Is the app available offline?
While the full features of our platform work best with an internet connection, we do offer basic offline functionality. You can download site information and maps ahead of time for access during your visit, even without connectivity.
How accurate is the historical information?
We pride ourselves on historical accuracy. All content is researched and verified by historians and cultural experts specializing in Tamil Nadu heritage. We regularly update our information based on the latest archaeological and historical findings.
Can I contribute information about a heritage site?
Absolutely! We welcome community contributions. You can submit information, corrections, or additional historical details through our contact form. Our team will review all submissions for accuracy before including them in our database.
Is there a mobile app available?
Currently, our platform is optimized for mobile browsers, providing a seamless experience on smartphones and tablets. A dedicated mobile app is under development and will be released soon with additional features such as AR (Augmented Reality) heritage exploration.
